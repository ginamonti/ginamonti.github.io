I am a student at Liberty University projected to graduate with the Application Development certification in October 2020. I have studied Visual Basic, C#, Java, and worked with a few IDEs. I am also studying Unity/C# development for games outside of classes.

I use Perl, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Angular to make code edits and Linux and Git Bash for server maintenance. I have experience managing databases with PGAdmin (SQL) and collaborating with other Evergreen developers.
